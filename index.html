About NVDS

Founded in 2003, Nepal Village Development Society (NVDS) is a grassroots NGO dedicated to uplifting rural communities across Nepal. We work on health, education, livelihoods, and renewable energy.

Our Causes

We combine immediate support with sustainable, community-led solutions.

Real Stories

Breaking Malnutrition

No child should go to bed hungry. We nourish bodies, minds, and futures by educating families on nutrition and kitchen gardens.

Pathways to Education

Education is the bridge from poverty to opportunity. We remove barriers by distributing books and upgrading classrooms.

Sustainable Energy

Clean energy powers hope. Our solar solutions reduce dependency on firewood and create safer, healthier homes.
